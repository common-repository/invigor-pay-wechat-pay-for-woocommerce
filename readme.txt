== Description ==

Allow your customers to make online purchases using their WeChat Pay account.


= Country Restriction =

This plugin is built for merchants in Singapore only.


== Installation ==

1. Login as an administrator in your wordpress site. In the navigation, hover to the "Plugins" menu then click "Add New".
1. Supply "Invigor Pay â€“ WeChat Pay for WooCommerce" in the search field.
1. The plugin will be displayed in the search result. Install and activate the plugin.
1. [Apply for a WeChat Pay account](https://www.invigorgroup.com/invigor-wechat-services-mobile-payments-and-advertising-for-retail/) with Invigor. Once your account is approved and setup, you will be issued public and private keys to access Invigor's Payment Gateway. *DO NOT* share the public and private keys with anyone. You can also create an account by reaching out to our team: wechatsg@invigorgroup.com
1. In Wordpress -> WooCommerce Settings -> Payment tab, modify the plugin settings providing the issued public and private keys accordingly.
1. After the above steps are done, you should be able to see "WeChat Pay" in the payment options during Order Checkout and use WeChat Pay for payments.
